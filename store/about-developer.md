# About the Developer

Used for Play Console developer info, App Store Connect review notes, or
any "about the developer" field. Two lengths below — pick whichever fits
the character limit.

## Short version (~50 words)

BeadSnap is built by a father-and-son team. This is our first Android app
and our first time publishing through Google Play Console. We wanted a
simple, safe, fully self-contained tool for designing fuse bead patterns
together — no ads, no accounts, nothing leaving the device.

## Longer version

BeadSnap started as a father-and-son project. We wanted an easy way to
turn photos and ideas into fuse bead patterns — the kind of craft project
you sit down and do together — without wrestling with an app full of ads,
accounts, and pop-ups. So we built our own.

This is our first time publishing to Google Play and the App Store, and
our first Android app. We've focused on making something genuinely safe
and contained: no data collection, no sign-in, everything stored right on
the device. What you see is what BeadSnap is — a small, careful tool made
by two people who wanted a better version of it to exist.
